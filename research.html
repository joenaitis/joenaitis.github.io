Research on Precipitation Relationships

I stored, processed, analyzed, and visualized complex sets of climate data by writing custom Python programs and creating maps in ESRI ArcGIS. My research provided evidence countering the traditional view of ENSO-precipitation relationships in the Altiplano of Peru and Bolivia and was published by the International Journal of Climatology.
