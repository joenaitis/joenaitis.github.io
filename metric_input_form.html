Metric Input Form Design and Maintenance

For over 1.5 years, I led the maintenance and design of our organization's metric input form in FormTitan and the storage of metrics in Salesforce with a team of staff and contractors.

As requested by the organization, the form operates both as a way for our global partners to input metrics but also functions as an outward-facing report and record of all our partners' submitted metrics (since our partners do have access to the organization's Salesforce instance). The form also allows for a partner to add their own custom metrics if desired.
